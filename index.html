Data Storage and Security

We are committed to the security of your highly sensitive financial information. We employ several industry-standard security measures to protect your data across all stages:

üîê A. Data Encryption

Encryption In Transit (Data moving off-device):

Any data transferred between your mobile device and our third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase Authentication, Google AdMob) is protected using Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL) encryption TLS/SSL. This ensures the data is encrypted and unreadable by unauthorized parties during transmission.

Encryption At Rest (Data stored on-device):

Your primary financial data (Transaction Data, Budget Information, etc.) stored locally on your device is protected using device-level encryption methods provided by the Android Operating System, leveraging technologies such as the Android Keystore System AES-256. This encrypts sensitive data at rest to protect it from unauthorized access if the device is lost or compromised.

üíæ B. Local and Cloud Storage Practices

Primary Storage:

All your core financial records are stored locally and exclusively on your device. By default, this data is NOT transferred to any ZEXO Ledger server.

User Control & Optional Cloud Backup:

You may optionally initiate a manual backup of your data to YOUR personal Google Drive account via the Google Drive API. These backup files are stored in your private cloud storage, and we do not retain keys or access to this stored data outside of the user-initiated backup/restore process.
